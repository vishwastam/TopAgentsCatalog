# AI Agents for Designers: 12 Tools to Supercharge Creativity

Design workflows are being transformed by agents that ideate, enhance, and iterate with you. Here are 12 tools that design teams are using in 2025:

1. **Midjourney** – Moodboards and visuals from text prompts.
2. **DALL·E 3** – Fills, edits, and creates stunning illustrations.
3. **Adobe Firefly** – Integrated with Photoshop for generative edits.
4. **Canva Magic Write** – Auto-suggests layout and copy.
5. **Runway Gen-2** – AI-generated short motion videos.
6. **Figma AI** – Layout optimization and component suggestions.
7. **Khroma** – Trains on your preferences to generate palettes.
8. **Remove.bg** – Background removal in one click.
9. **Let's Enhance** – Upscales low-res assets.
10. **Fontjoy** – Font pairing suggestions.
11. **LogoAI** – Generates logo ideas from brand inputs.
12. **Uizard** – Converts wireframes into functional UIs.

Designers are speeding up concept creation by 50–70% using these tools in the loop.
